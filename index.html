About Me

Hi, I'm Ksenia

I'm a passionate artist and creative professional dedicated to pushing the boundaries of visual expression. My work spans across paintings, sketches, mixed media, and illustrations — always driven by a desire to evoke emotion and tell compelling stories.

When I'm not in the studio, you'll find me traveling the world, drawing inspiration from diverse cultures, landscapes, and people. Every journey feeds my creativity and finds its way into my art.
