# Images Folder — KS Art Website

Place your images here with these filenames:

## Homepage
- `artist-portrait.jpg` — Your artist photo (about section, ~500x600px)
- `work-1.jpg` through `work-8.jpg` — Portfolio pieces (~600x400px each)

## Blog
- `blog-1.jpg` through `blog-6.jpg` — Blog post thumbnails (~600x400px each)

## Travel Blog
- `travel-featured.jpg` — Featured travel post hero image (~800x500px)
- `travel-1.jpg` through `travel-6.jpg` — Travel post thumbnails (~600x400px each)

> **Note:** The site uses Unsplash placeholder images as fallbacks, so it will look great even before you add your own photos. Simply drop your images in this folder with the correct names and they'll automatically replace the placeholders.
